About Us

Reflections Dance Academy provides a carefully designed and
progressive program in dance technique and competitive dance forms,
which provides the opportunity for all dancers to grow in their dance
knowledge and skill. Our professional staff has many years of experience
and expertise in teaching all ages and levels. The staff understands dance
disciplines must be taught carefully with enjoyment and encouragement.

Our purpose is to train and educate dancers, while retaining the joy of
dance itself. Whether your goal is to dance recreationally or to compete;
Our "Dance+ Academy" will provide the environment, encouragement
and faculty to help you achieve your goals
